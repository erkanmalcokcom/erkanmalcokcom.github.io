Normal Distribution  [draft]

The normal distribution, also known as the Gaussian distribution or bell curve, is a continuous probability distribution that is widely used in statistics, mathematics, and natural sciences. The normal distribution is characterized by its probability density function (PDF), which has a symmetric bell-shaped curve with a single peak at the mean value.
